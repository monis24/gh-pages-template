Medicare Beneficiary Identifiers (MBIs) are crucial for identifying individuals enrolled in Medicare. These Medicare numbers follow a specific format, consisting of 11 characters divided into three groups. Valid examples include 1AB2-CD3-EF45 and 9HJ1-NP9-RT99. These unique identifiers are essential for processing Medicare claims and managing beneficiary information.

The Medicare Beneficiary Number format allows for various combinations of letters and numbers while maintaining a consistent structure. Additional valid examples include 5RT7-YA8-BC12, 3DE4-FG5-HJ67, 7KL8-MN9-PQ23, 4EF5-GH6-JK34, 8LM9-NP1-QR78, and 1TU2-VW3-XY91. These MBIs demonstrate the range of possible combinations while adhering to the required format for Medicare identification numbers.
