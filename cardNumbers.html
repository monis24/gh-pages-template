Credit Card Numbers

American Express

American Express cards typically start with 34 or 37 and have 15 digits. Valid formats include 3712-345678-12345, 3412 345678 12345, and 371234567812345. These numbers are crucial for processing transactions and verifying card authenticity.

However, not all similar-looking numbers are valid American Express cards. For instance, 4111111111111111, 5500000000000004, and 361449635398432 are not valid American Express numbers. It's important to distinguish between valid and invalid formats to prevent errors in payment processing.

Mastercard

Mastercard numbers usually begin with 51-55 or 2221-2720 and contain 16 digits. Examples of valid Mastercard numbers include 5555555555554444, 2221000000000009, and 5105-1051-0510-5100. These numbers play a vital role in secure online and offline transactions.

It's crucial to recognize invalid Mastercard formats. For example, 4111111111111111, 371449635398431, and 555555555555444 are not valid Mastercard numbers. Proper validation helps prevent fraudulent transactions and ensures smooth payment processing.

Visa

Visa card numbers start with 4 and are 16 digits long (13 digits for older cards). Valid Visa numbers include 4111111111111111, 4012-8888-8888-1881, and 4222 2222 2222 2121. These numbers are essential for identifying Visa cards in various payment systems.

Not all 16-digit numbers starting with 4 are valid Visa cards. For instance, 411111111111111, 4111-1111-1111-11, and 41234567890123456 are invalid formats. Proper validation is crucial for maintaining payment security and accuracy.

Diners Club

Diners Club card numbers typically start with 30, 36, or 38 and are 14 digits long. Valid formats include 300420688846-75, 30366806859700, and 3864 5881.3882.84. These unique numbers help distinguish Diners Club cards in payment processing systems.

It's important to note that not all similar numbers are valid Diners Club cards. For example, 3056930902590, 3056-9309-0259-0, and 3056 9309 0259 044 are invalid formats. Proper validation ensures accurate transaction processing for Diners Club cards.

JCB

Not all 16-digit numbers starting with 35 are valid JCB cards. For instance, 353011133300000, 3530-1113-3330-00, and 3412345678901234 are invalid formats. Proper validation of JCB numbers is essential for secure and accurate payment processing.
