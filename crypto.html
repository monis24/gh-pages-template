Ethereum Addresses

Positive Test Cases

Ethereum addresses play a crucial role in the world of cryptocurrency. These unique identifiers are essential for sending and receiving Ether and other tokens on the Ethereum blockchain. A typical Ethereum address, such as 0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc454e4438f44e, consists of 42 characters, including the '0x' prefix. Another example of a valid Ethereum address is 0xA0b86991c6218b36c1d19D4a2e9Eb0cE3606eB48, which demonstrates the mix of lowercase and uppercase letters commonly found in these addresses.

The structure of Ethereum addresses is designed to ensure uniqueness and security. For instance, 0x1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef12345678 and 0xABCDEF1234567890ABCDEF1234567890ABCDEF12 are both valid Ethereum addresses, showcasing the range of characters that can be used. It's worth noting that even addresses with repeated characters, such as 0x000000000000000000000000000000000000000A or 0xffffffffffffffffffffffffffffffffffffffff, are valid and can be used as wallet addresses on the Ethereum network.

Bitcoin Addresses

Positive Test Cases

Bitcoin addresses are fundamental to the Bitcoin network, serving as unique identifiers for sending and receiving Bitcoin. A standard Bitcoin address, such as 1BvBMSEYstWetqTFn5Au4m4GFg7xJaNVN2, typically consists of 26-35 alphanumeric characters. Another example of a valid Bitcoin address is 3J98t1WpEZ73CNmQviecrnyiWrnqRhWNLy, which demonstrates a different format starting with '3', indicating a script hash address.

Bitcoin addresses can take various forms, each with its own characteristics. For instance, 1KFHE7w8BhaENAswwryaoccDb9fT1fhwBq and 3Nxwenay9Z8Lc9JBiTB1rkD8jZPMgX5oKB are both valid addresses, showcasing the diversity in their structure. It's worth noting that some Bitcoin addresses hold historical significance, such as 1A1zP1eP5QGefi2DMPTfTL5SLmv7DivfNa, which is believed to be Satoshi Nakamoto's address from the genesis block. Other valid examples include 3D2oetdNuZUqQHPJmcMDDHYoqkyNVsFk9r and 3P3QsMVK89JBNqZQv5zMAKG8FK3kJM4rjq, demonstrating the range of possible Bitcoin addresses.
