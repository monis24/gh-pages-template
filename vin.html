Vehicle Identification Number

Formatted

Formatted Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) typically include hyphens to separate different sections of the code. Valid examples include ABC-12345-D-67890123 and 123-ABCDE-F-12345678. These formats make it easier to read and identify the different components of the VIN, such as the manufacturer identifier, vehicle attributes, and serial number. Other valid formatted VINs include XYZ-98765-G-43210987 and 789-HJNPR-Z-09876543.

Additional examples of correctly formatted VINs are DEF-56789-H-23456789, 456-JNPRZ-J-34567890, and GHJ-01234-K-45678901. The format typically consists of four groups separated by hyphens, with the third group being a single character. Other valid examples include NPR-67890-L-56789012, STU-23456-M-67890123, and VWX-78901-N-78901234, all following the same pattern.

Invalid formatted VINs often have incorrect lengths or character placements. For instance, ABC-12345-D-6789012 is too short in the last section, while 123-ABCDE-F-123456789 is too long. Examples like XYZ-9876-G-43210987 have an incorrectly short second section. VINs containing invalid characters, such as 789-HJNPR-Z-0987654I, are also not valid.

Other invalid formatted VINs include those with incorrect characters in specific positions, like DEF-56789-O-23456789 and 456-JNPRZ-Q-34567890, where 'O' and 'Q' are not typically used in VINs. Examples with incorrect segment lengths, such as GHJ-01234-K-4567890 or NPR-678-L-5678901A, are invalid. Additionally, VINs with extra hyphens (STU-23456-M-6789012-3) or spaces (VWX-78901-N-7890 1234) do not conform to the standard format.

Unformatted

Unformatted Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) appear as a continuous string of 17 characters without any separators. Valid examples include ABC12345D67890123 and 123ABCDEF12345678. These unformatted VINs contain the same information as their formatted counterparts but without the visual separation. Other correct unformatted VINs are XYZ98765G43210987 and 789HJNPRZ09876543.

Additional instances of valid unformatted VINs include DEF56789H23456789, 456JNPRZJ34567890, and GHJ01234K45678901. The absence of hyphens doesn't affect the validity of the VIN as long as all 17 characters are present and in the correct order. Other examples of correct unformatted VINs are NPR67890L56789012, STU23456M67890123, and VWX78901N78901234.

Invalid unformatted VINs often have incorrect lengths or character compositions. For example, ABC12345D6789012 is too short at 16 characters, while 123ABCDEF123456789 is too long at 18 characters. VINs with an incorrect number of characters in specific sections, like XYZ9876G43210987, are also invalid. The presence of invalid characters, such as in 789HJNPRZ0987654I, renders a VIN incorrect.

Other examples of invalid unformatted VINs include those with characters that are not typically used in VINs, such as DEF56789O23456789 and 456JNPRZQ34567890, where 'O' and 'Q' are present. VINs that are too short, like GHJ01234K4567890, or contain non-alphanumeric characters, such as NPR678L5678901A and STU23456M6789012-3, are invalid. Additionally, VINs with spaces, like VWX78901N7890 1234, do not conform to the standard unformatted VIN structure.
